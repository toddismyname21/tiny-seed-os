## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity
Pittsburgh's local food market is **underserved but educated**. The city has a strong farm-to-table culture built by Penn's Corner Farm Alliance over 20+ years, yet individual farms struggle with marketing bandwidth. Your competitors are strong on product but weak on digital engagement and automation.

### Your Edge
- **Technology**: Tiny Seed OS gives you infrastructure competitors don't have
- **Speed**: "Field Mode" content creation in 45 seconds vs their hours
- **Data**: Real-time inventory tied to marketing = no overselling, smart promotions
- **Automation**: Triggers that run while you're in the field
