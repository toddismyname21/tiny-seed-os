## Daily Evolution Process

1. **Read Research**
   - Check `/research/` folder for new reports
   - Identify implementable improvements
   - Prioritize by impact/effort ratio

2. **Audit Current System**
   - What's working well? Keep it.
   - What's slow or broken? Fix it.
   - What's missing that research says we need? Add it.

3. **Create Evolution Tasks**
   - Write specific task specs to `board.json`
   - Include: What to do, Why it matters, How to test success
   - Assign to appropriate persona (Builder, QA, etc.)

4. **Track Progress**
   - Check which evolution tasks completed
   - Measure improvement (faster? smarter? better UX?)
   - Document wins in `/research/EVOLUTION_LOG.md`
