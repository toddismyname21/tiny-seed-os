## 9. DEBT COLLECTION GUIDANCE (Pennsylvania)

### Key Pennsylvania Debt Collection Information

- **Statute of Limitations:** 4 years for most debts (runs from last payment or date debt became past due)
- **Judgment Validity:** 20 years, but must be revived every 5 years to remain enforceable
- **Judgment Liens:** Can only be placed on real property in Pennsylvania
- **Recording:** File judgment with Court of Common Pleas in county where debtor owns property
