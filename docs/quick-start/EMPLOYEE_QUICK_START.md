**URL:** `employee.html` (save to your phone's home screen)

## GETTING STARTED

### First Login
1. Open the app
2. Enter your **4-digit PIN** (given by manager)
3. You're in!

### Adding to Your Phone
**iPhone:** Share button > Add to Home Screen
**Android:** Menu (3 dots) > Add to Home Screen

---

## DAILY ROUTINE

### 1. CLOCK IN (Start of Shift)
- Open the app
- Make sure you're **at the farm** (GPS required)
- Tap the big **CLOCK IN** button
- You'll see your start time

### 2. VIEW YOUR TASKS
- Go to **Tasks** tab
- See what needs to be done today
- Tasks show location and priority

### 3. COMPLETE TASKS
- Tap a task to see details
- Do the work
- Tap **COMPLETE**
- Add notes if needed

### 4. LOG HARVESTS
When you harvest:
- Go to **Harvest** tab
- Tap **Log Harvest**
- Pick the crop
- Enter pounds or count
- Submit

### 5. CLOCK OUT (End of Shift)
- Tap **CLOCK OUT**
- Your hours are saved automatically

---

## REPORTING PROBLEMS

### See Pests or Disease?
1. Go to **Scout** tab
2. Tap **Report Issue**
3. Take a photo
4. Select what you saw
5. Submit

### Can't Complete a Task?
- Tap the task
- Add a note explaining why
- Tell your Field Lead

---

## QUICK TIPS

- Clock in **every day** - no clock in = no pay record
- Complete tasks as you go - don't wait until end of day
- Log harvests **immediately** after picking
- Take photos of any problems you find
- Ask your Field Lead if you're unsure about anything
