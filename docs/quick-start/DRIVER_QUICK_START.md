# DRIVER QUICK START GUIDE
## Tiny Seed Farm - Delivery App Guide

---

## Your App: Driver App

**URL:** `web_app/driver.html` (save to your phone's home screen)

---

## GETTING STARTED

### Login
1. Open the app
2. Enter your **4-digit PIN**
3. Today's route appears

---

## DELIVERY DAY

### 1. START YOUR DAY
- Login to see your route
- Check total stops
- Note any special instructions
- Start your route

### 2. AT EACH STOP

**Before leaving truck:**
- Check customer name
- Check what items they get
- Note special instructions

**Delivery:**
- Find the customer
- Deliver their items
- Complete documentation (see below)

### 3. DOCUMENT EVERY DELIVERY

You MUST:
1. **Take a photo** of delivered items
2. **Get signature** (if required)
3. **Mark as complete**

**How:**
- Tap the stop
- Tap **Complete Delivery**
- Take photo
- Get signature
- Submit

### 4. NAVIGATE TO NEXT STOP
- Tap next stop
- Tap **Navigate**
- Follow directions

### 5. REPORT ISSUES IMMEDIATELY

If there's a problem:
- Tap **Report Issue**
- Select the problem type:
  - Customer not home
  - Wrong address
  - Damaged items
  - Other
- Add notes
- Take photo if needed
- Submit

---

## SWITCHING TO EMPLOYEE MODE

If you also do farm work:
1. Go to Settings
2. Toggle **Employee Mode**
3. Access time clock, tasks, harvests
4. Toggle back for deliveries

---

## IMPORTANT RULES

- **Never skip proof of delivery** - photos protect everyone
- **Report all issues** - don't just leave items
- **Follow route order** - optimized for efficiency
- **Keep phone charged** - you need GPS
