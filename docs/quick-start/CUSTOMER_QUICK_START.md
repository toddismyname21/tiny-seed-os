## Your Portal: Customer Portal

**URL:** `web_app/customer.html`

## GETTING STARTED

### First Time Access
1. Go to the Customer Portal URL
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Click **Send Magic Link**
4. Check your email (and spam folder)
5. Click the link in the email
6. You're logged in!

### Returning Customers
- Your session stays active for a while
- If logged out, just request a new magic link
- No password to remember!

---

## PLACING AN ORDER

### 1. BROWSE PRODUCTS
- See what's currently available
- Products show:
  - Name and description
  - Price per unit
  - Available quantity
  - Photo (when available)

### 2. ADD TO CART
- Click **Add to Cart**
- Adjust quantity if needed
- Keep browsing or checkout

### 3. REVIEW CART
- Click the cart icon
- See all items
- Adjust quantities
- Remove items if needed
- See total price

### 4. CHECKOUT
- Confirm your items
- Select pickup/delivery option
- Choose date/time
- Add any special instructions
- Confirm order

### 5. CONFIRMATION
- You'll see order confirmation
- Email receipt sent automatically
- Save your order number

## DELIVERY ORDERS

If delivery is available to your area:
1. Select delivery at checkout
2. Confirm your address
3. Choose delivery window
4. Be available to receive
5. Driver will confirm delivery

## ORDER STATUS

What each status means:
- **Pending** - We received it, processing soon
- **Confirmed** - Order accepted, being prepared
- **Ready** - Ready for pickup
- **Out for Delivery** - Driver on the way
- **Completed** - You have your items!
