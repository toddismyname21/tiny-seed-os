# Timeline: Todd Wilson & Kretschmann Farm
## Chronological History of the Relationship

**Source:** 247 emails from Don Lease History archive + documents
**Compiled:** 2026-01-16

---

## Pre-Relationship Era

### 1970s
- Don & Becky Kretschmann begin farming at 257 Zeigler Rd, Rochester, PA
- Start of organic farming journey

### 1970s-2020 (50 Years)
- Kretschmann Farm becomes established organic CSA
- Builds loyal customer base in Pittsburgh area
- Develops 80 acres of certified organic farmland
- Establishes apple orchard (organic)

---

## Todd's Employment Era

### ~2013-2014
- **Todd Wilson works as employee at Kretschmann Farm**
- Learns organic farming practices
- Develops relationship with Don & Becky

### 2014
- **Todd leaves to start Tiny Seed Farm independently**
- Operates separate business
- Supplies restaurants in Pittsburgh
- Builds reputation for baby greens, specialty crops

---

## Kretschmann Retirement & Todd's Return

### 2020
- **Don & Becky retire from active farming after 2020 season**
- Begin searching for successor farmers
- Maria (daughter) takes over orchard/cidery operations

### Late 2022 (December)
- **Todd approaches Don about leasing land**
- Email Dec 15, 2022: Todd asks about "template-like thing to formalize my use and care of your fields"
- Don responds with initial field offerings:
  - O Field: 0.5 acres
  - F3M Field: 0.5 acres
  - JS3 Field: 0.35 acres
- Discussion of apple orchard access begins
- Don mentions "beta lease" and "schemas for machinery use valuations"
- Don expresses desire to "make it fair and transparent"

### December 2022
- Todd expresses interest in mentor/mentee grant program
- Discusses soil testing plans
- Apple arrangement discussed (complex due to Maria's cider business)

---

## Active Leasing Period

### 2023 (Year 1)
- **First full season of Todd leasing from Don**
- Approximately 5 acres under cultivation
- July 2023: Don sends first formal invoice/bill
  - Includes electric (10% share), equipment percentages, fuel costs
  - Apple arrangement still being worked out
- Soil tests conducted (Logan Labs, March 2023)
- Don maintains organic certification, Todd provides documentation

### 2024 (Year 2)
- **Continued operations, ~5 acres**
- April 2024: Todd requests additional field for fall brassicas
- Don provides guidance on cover crops, field rotation
- April 2024: Don requests seed/input documentation for organic certification
- September 2024: Don writes "A plea for the future of our farm" letter
  - Describes Maria's struggles with cidery
  - Mentions Todd leasing "about 5 A"
  - First public mention of land trust plans
- October 2024: Discussion of H2A visa workers, Business Builder Grant
- Soil tests conducted (Waypoint Analytical, October 2024)
- December 2024: Don shares organic certification info with Todd

### 2025 (Year 3)
- **Expansion to ~12 acres**
- January 2025: Don sends mass email to former CSA members endorsing Todd
  - States farm will go to "land trust"
  - Todd will have "long-term lease"
  - "Highest endorsement" of Todd's work
- January 2025: Migrant worker housing discussed ($12,000/year)
- February 2025: CSA proposal discussions with churches, former customers
- April 2025: Don sends "leasing spreadsheet" (current terms)
- Maria decides to step back from orchard
- Apples to be included in Tiny Seed CSA going forward
