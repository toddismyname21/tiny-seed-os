## Comparison to Industry Standards

### Typical Farm Land Lease Rates (PA/Midwest)
- Cropland cash rent: $50-200/acre/year
- Vegetable land (prime): $200-500/acre/year
- Orchard land: Varies widely

### Typical Lease Structures
- **Cash Rent:** Fixed $/acre
- **Crop Share:** Landowner gets % of harvest
- **Hybrid:** Lower cash rent + some product sharing

### Kretschmann Arrangement
Appears to be a **hybrid cost-sharing model** rather than traditional cash rent:
- Shared infrastructure costs (electric, equipment, gas)
- Field access provided
- Long-term security via land trust
- Landowner provides marketing support

**Assessment:** This arrangement seems designed to **reduce Todd's startup costs** while maintaining the farm's viability. It's more **partnership-oriented** than a typical tenant-landlord relationship.
