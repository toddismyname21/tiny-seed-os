**Key Organizations:**

| Organization | What They Do | Why It Matters |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|

**Key Statistics:**
- 400 million acres will change hands in the next decade
- Half a million farmers expected to retire by 2030
- Most senior farmers don't have succession plans
- Non-family succession is increasing as farmers age

**What Works:**
- Early planning (3-5 years before transition)
- Written agreements (not handshakes)
- Professional advisors (legal, tax, financial)
- Clear communication of expectations
- Beginning farmer tax credits (some states)

### 3. Land Trust Deep Dive

**The Agrarian Commons Model:**

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Lease term | 99 years |
| Rent setting | Collaborative, based on farm viability |
| Governance | Board includes farmers |
| Inheritance | Can pass to chosen successor |
| Protection | Land permanently protected from development |

**How It Works:**
1. Landowner donates/sells land to land trust
2. Land trust issues long-term lease to farmer
3. Rent stays affordable (non-extractive)
4. Farmer has security to invest
5. Land stays agricultural forever

**Tax Benefits for Don:**

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Income tax deduction | 100% of income for farmers, 15-year carryforward |
| Estate tax reduction | Lower property value = lower estate taxes |
| Up to $500,000 exclusion | For land under conservation easement |
| USDA matching | ACEP-ALE pays up to 50% of easement value |

**This is exactly what Don said he wants.** He told CSA members: "the farm will remain in a land trust and Todd will have a long-term lease."

**Sources:**
- [Agrarian Trust FAQ](https://www.agrariantrust.org/initiatives/agrarian-commons/frequently-asked-questions/)
- [Equitable Lease Model](https://farmlandaccess.org/land-trusts/agrarian-commons-guide/equitable-lease/)
- [Land Trust Alliance Tax Incentives](https://landtrustalliance.org/resources/learn/explore/income-tax-incentives-for-land-conservation)
