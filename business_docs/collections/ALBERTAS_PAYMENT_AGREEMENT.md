## DEFAULT

### Definition of Default
A default occurs if:
1. Any payment is more than **3 business days** late, OR
2. Any payment is made for less than the scheduled amount without prior written agreement

### Consequences of Default
Upon default, the **entire remaining balance becomes immediately due and payable**, and Creditor may, without further notice:

1. File a claim in the Beaver County Magisterial District Court (Small Claims) for the full remaining balance plus court costs and filing fees
2. Report the debt to commercial credit bureaus
3. Turn the account over to a collections agency
4. Pursue all other legal remedies available under Pennsylvania law

### Waiver of Further Notice
By signing this agreement, Debtor waives any right to additional notice or demand before Creditor takes action upon default.

## ADDITIONAL TERMS

### No Future Credit
Until this balance is paid in full, Debtor shall not receive any products from Tiny Seed Farm on credit. Any future orders must be prepaid or paid upon delivery.

### Entire Agreement
