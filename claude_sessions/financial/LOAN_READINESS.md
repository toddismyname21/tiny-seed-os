## PART 2: OPERATING LOAN (CSA Capital)

### What It Covers
- Seeds and supplies
- Labor (early season)
- Equipment maintenance
- Marketing/CSA signup costs
- Operating expenses until CSA payments come in

### Typical Terms (Farm Credit)
| Feature | Typical Range |
|---------|---------------|
| Amount | $10,000 - $100,000 |
| Interest Rate | 6-9% |
| Term | 12 months (renewed annually) |
| Collateral | Farm assets, crops, equipment |

### What They'll Ask
- How much do you need?
- What will you use it for? (be specific)
- How will you repay it? (CSA income timeline)
- What's your backup plan?

## PART 7: RED FLAGS TO AVOID

**Don't:**
- Underestimate your debt (they'll verify)
- Overestimate your income
- Apply to multiple lenders simultaneously (hurts credit)
- Miss any payments between now and application

**Do:**
- Be completely honest
- Show you understand your numbers
- Demonstrate a plan
- Ask questions
