## WHAT IS AN FSA MICROLOAN?

FSA Microloans are designed for small and beginning farmers with:
- Simplified application process
- Reduced paperwork
- Less stringent requirements than traditional farm loans
- No requirement for 3 years of production history

### Two Types Available:

**1. Operating Microloans** (up to $50,000)
- Seeds, fertilizer, supplies
- Livestock feed
- Farm equipment
- Minor repairs
- Family living expenses
- Refinancing farm debt

**2. Farm Ownership Microloans** (up to $50,000)
- Land purchase
- Construction/repairs
- Conservation improvements

## FSA vs FARM CREDIT COMPARISON

| Factor | FSA Microloan | Farm Credit |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Max Amount | $50,000 | $100,000+ |
| Interest Rate | ~4.875% | 6-9% |
| Application | Simplified | Standard |
| Approval Time | 60 days | 1-3 weeks |
| Paperwork | Reduced | Full package |
| Beginning Farmer Priority | **YES** | Some programs |

**Recommendation:** Apply to FSA first (better rates, beginning farmer priority). Farm Credit as backup.
