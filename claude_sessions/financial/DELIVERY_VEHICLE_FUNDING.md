## OPTION 1: FSA FARM STORAGE FACILITY LOAN (FSFL)

**Best for: Refrigerated delivery vehicles**

The FSFL program was amended to include:
- Portable storage structures
- Portable equipment
- **Storage and handling trucks** (including refrigerated)

### Details
| Feature | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Max Loan | $100,000 for trucks |
| Down Payment | Reduced for microloans |
| Term | Up to 7 years |
| Rate | Low (FSA rates) |

### Why This Works
A refrigerated delivery vehicle qualifies as a "storage and handling truck" - it keeps produce at proper temperature during transport to chefs and markets.

### How to Apply
1. Contact Butler County FSA: 724-482-4800 ext 2
2. Request FSFL application for storage/handling truck
3. Provide vehicle specifications and quotes

Source: [FSA Farm Storage Facility Loan Program](https://www.fsa.usda.gov/resources/programs/farm-storage-facility-loan-fsfl-program)

## VEHICLE TYPES TO CONSIDER

### Refrigerated Van/Truck
**Best for:** Wholesale deliveries to chefs, market transport
- Maintains temperature during transport
- Required for food safety compliance
- Higher resale value

### Cargo Van
**Best for:** Local deliveries, farmers markets
- Lower cost than refrigerated
- More versatile
- Easier to find used

### Box Truck
**Best for:** Large volume, multiple stops
- More cargo space
- Better for wholesale routes
- Higher fuel costs

## RECOMMENDED APPROACH

### Step 1: Determine Needs
- What size vehicle?
- Refrigeration needed?
- New or used acceptable?

- Visit dealers for pricing

- Document specifications

### Step 3: Apply to FSA First
1. Call Butler FSA: 724-482-4800 ext 2
2. Ask about FSFL for refrigerated vehicle OR
