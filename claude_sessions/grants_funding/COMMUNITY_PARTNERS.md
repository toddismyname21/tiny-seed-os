## NOTES

**Community Engagement Best Practices:**
1. Maintain regular contact (quarterly minimum) with key partners
2. Update partners on farm progress and new initiatives
3. Look for mutual benefit in all partnerships
4. Document all significant interactions
5. Express gratitude - handwritten notes, farm produce gifts
6. Invite partners to farm events and tours

**Mission Alignment Focus Areas:**
- Food access and food security
- Community building
- Environmental stewardship
- Agricultural education
- Supporting local economy
