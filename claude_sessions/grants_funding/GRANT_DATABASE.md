## ROLLING/ANNUAL PROGRAMS

### EQIP (Environmental Quality Incentives Program)
- **Agency:** NRCS
- **Type:** Cost-share (covers 75-90% of costs)
- **Purpose:** Conservation practices, high tunnels, irrigation
- **Beginning Farmer Benefit:** Up to 90% cost-share, 50% upfront
- **Requirements:** Tax ID, property deed/lease, farm number
- **Apply:** Local NRCS office
- **Website:** [NRCS EQIP](https://www.nrcs.usda.gov/programs-initiatives/environmental-quality-incentives-program)

### CSP (Conservation Stewardship Program)
- **Agency:** NRCS
- **Purpose:** Rewards existing good stewardship practices
- **Focus:** Soil quality, water conservation, biodiversity, wildlife habitat
- **New:** Combined with EQIP under Regenerative Pilot Program ($700M investment)
- **Apply:** Local NRCS office

### REAP (Rural Energy for America Program)
- **Purpose:** Energy improvements for farms
- **Grant Amounts:**
  - Renewable energy: $2,500 - $1,000,000
  - Energy efficiency: $1,500 - $500,000
- **Eligibility:** Rural area (population <50,000), >50% electricity for business
- **Cannot Have:** Back taxes, delinquent debt
- **Note:** Taxable income (1099-G issued)
- **Website:** [USDA Rural Development REAP](https://www.rd.usda.gov)

### Farm Storage Facility Loan Program (FSFL)
- **Purpose:** Cold storage, wash/pack stations
- **Special:** Small vegetable producers eligible for insurance waiver
- **Good For:** CSAs, diversified producers
- **Apply:** Farm Service Agency (FSA)

### SARE Northeast Grants
- **Purpose:** Research and education on sustainable practices
- **PA Funding History:** $13.4M across 311 projects
- **Types:** Research/education, professional development, producer-led
- **Not For:** Starting a farm (for established farms testing new ideas)
- **Website:** [SARE](https://www.nesare.org)
