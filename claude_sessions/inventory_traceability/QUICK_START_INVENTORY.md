## Speed Tips

### Do It Fast
- Don't overthink item names
- "Thing that does X" is fine
- Round value estimates
- Skip items worth <$10 unless many

### Batch by Area
- Finish one area completely
- Then move to next
- Don't backtrack

### Group Similar Items
- "Box of fittings - est. 100 pieces - $200 total"
- Don't photograph every fitting

### Condition Shorthand
- **G** = Good (works fine)
- **F** = Fair (works but worn)
- **P** = Poor (barely works)
- **R** = Needs Repair

## What NOT to Inventory

Skip these (for now):
- Individual screws/nails
- Broken/disposed items
- Items you're selling
- Personal items on farm
- Crops in ground (separate tracking)

---

## Minimum Info Needed

**Absolute minimum per item:**
1. Photo
2. Name
3. Location
4. Quantity

Everything else is bonus.
