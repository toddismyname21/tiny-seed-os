## Minimum Info Per Item

1. **Photo** - Snap it
2. **Name** - What is it?
3. **Quantity** - How many?
4. **Location** - Where is it?

Everything else is bonus. Get the basics, refine later.

## Skip These

- Individual small items (<$10)
- Broken items being disposed
- Personal belongings
- Crops in ground (separate system)
