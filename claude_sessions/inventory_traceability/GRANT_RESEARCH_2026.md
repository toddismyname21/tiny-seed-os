## ROLLING/ONGOING PROGRAMS

### 5. NRCS High Tunnel Initiative (EQIP)
**Source:** USDA NRCS
**Amount:** ~$7,670/year (up to 90% for beginning farmers)
**Deadline:** Rolling (contact local NRCS)
**Eligibility:** Farms wanting to extend growing season

#### Why This Grant
High tunnels = year-round production = more CSA value. Beginning farmers get 90% cost-share with 50% upfront.

#### How to Apply
1. Contact PA NRCS: Dayanna Barnes 717-237-2218 or Dan Ludwig 717-237-2256
2. Complete AD 1026 (land suitability form)
3. File CPA 1200 application
4. Install NEW structure from commercial source

#### Requirements
- Crops must be grown in natural soil or raised beds (max 12" depth)
- No permanent walkways
- Manufacturer installation specs followed

#### Priority: **HIGH** - Extend growing season, boost CSA income

#### Priority: **HIGH** - Fast, flexible capital

### 10. Organic Farming Research Foundation (OFRF) Grant
**Source:** OFRF
**Amount:** Up to $20,000
**Deadline:** LOI typically opens **July** (watch for 2026 announcement)
**Eligibility:** Farmers on certified organic land

#### Why This Grant
Must be farmer-led or meaningfully involve farmers. Perfect for testing new practices on certified organic land. BIPOC applicants strongly encouraged.

#### How to Apply
1. Visit [OFRF](https://ofrf.org/research/grant-awards/)
2. Submit Letter of Intent (6-8 week window)
3. If selected, full proposal due December
4. Awards announced April/May

#### Requirements
- Must take place on certified organic land (Tiny Seed qualifies!)
- Address climate resilience OR economic/social considerations
- Include education/outreach component
- Measurable outcomes required

#### Priority: **HIGH** - Research funding for established organic farm

## CARBON CREDIT PROGRAMS

### 13. Indigo Ag Carbon Program
**Source:** Indigo Agriculture
**Amount:** ~$15/credit (0.2-1 credits per acre in year 1)
**Deadline:** Ongoing enrollment
**Eligibility:** Farms practicing regenerative agriculture

#### Why This Grant
Annual payments for carbon sequestration. Farmers receive 75% of carbon credit value. Payments increase over time as practices mature.

#### How to Apply
1. Visit [Indigo Ag](https://www.indigoag.com/carbon-credits)
2. Provide 3-5 years of farm data (crops, yields, practices)
3. Implement qualifying practices (cover crops, reduced tillage)
4. Receive annual payments

#### Estimated Income
- 5.6 acres x 0.5 credits x $15 = ~$42-$84/year (increases over time)
- Better suited for larger operations, but establishes baseline

#### Priority: **LOW** - Small acreage limits returns, but worth monitoring
