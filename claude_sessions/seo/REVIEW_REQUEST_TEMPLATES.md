## BEST PRACTICES

### When to Send
| Trigger | Wait Time | Template |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| After 3rd pickup | Same day | Template 1 |
| After renewal | Within 24 hours | Template 2 |
| After positive feedback | Immediately | Template 4 |
| After referral signup | Within 24 hours | Template 5 |
| End of season | Final week | Template 6 |
| Mid-season (new members) | Week 8-10 | Template 7 |

### How Many to Request
- **Week 1-4:** 5 requests
- **Week 5-8:** 5 requests
- **Week 9-12:** 5 requests
- **End of season:** Send to all remaining members

### Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Personalize with their name and specific details
- Make it easy with a direct link
- Send at convenient times (Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM)
- Thank them regardless of whether they leave a review
- Respond to every review within 24 hours

**DON'T:**
- Offer incentives (violates Google TOS)
- Send more than 2 requests per member per season
- Mass email your entire list at once (looks suspicious)
- Ask for "5-star" reviews specifically (ask for "honest" reviews)
- Stress about negative reviews (respond gracefully, learn from them)

## GETTING YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW LINK

1. Go to Google Business Profile Manager
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy the short link provided
4. Use a URL shortener if needed (bit.ly, etc.)

Direct format: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR_PLACE_ID]/review`
