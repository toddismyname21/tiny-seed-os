## CONTENT PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### For Each Blog Post:
1. **Research** - Check keyword volume, competitor content
2. **Outline** - H2/H3 structure with keywords
3. **Write** - 1,000-1,500 words minimum
4. **Optimize** - Title, meta description, alt text
5. **Add Schema** - Article schema markup
6. **Publish** - Submit to Search Console
7. **Promote** - Social media, email, etc.
8. **Track** - Monitor rankings monthly
