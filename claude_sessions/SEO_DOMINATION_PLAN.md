## THE THREE PILLARS OF LOCAL SEO

Google's local algorithm ranks on THREE factors:

### 1. RELEVANCE (What you do)
- Business category accuracy
- Service descriptions
- Website content matching search intent

### 2. DISTANCE (Where you are)
- Physical location to searcher
- Service area definitions
- Multiple pickup locations = multiple signals

### 3. PROMINENCE (How well-known you are)
- Review quantity and quality
- Citations and backlinks
- Brand mentions online
- Content authority
