## CRITICAL TIMING WINDOWS

### Can't Miss Dates

| Window | Task | Consequence if Missed |
|--------|------|----------------------|
| Dec 1-Jan 15 | Start lisianthus | No summer lisianthus |
| Feb 15-Mar 15 | Split dahlias | Late/weak plants |
| Jul 15-Aug 1 | Seed overwinter snaps/stock | No early spring flowers |
| Sep 1-15 | Transplant overwinters | Poor establishment |
| Oct 1-15 | Direct seed sweet peas | Won't overwinter |
| After first frost | Dig dahlias | Tuber loss to freeze |

### Weather-Dependent Triggers

| Trigger | Task | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| Soil 50°F | Transplant warm crops | Use soil thermometer |
| Soil 60°F | Direct seed beans/corn | Warmer = better |
| First frost warning | Cover tender crops | Or harvest |
| Hard freeze (<28°F) | Apply winter protection | Overwinter crops |
| Day length <12 hrs | Stop dahlia feeding | ~Sep 21 |
