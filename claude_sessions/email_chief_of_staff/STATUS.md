### 10. True Autonomy (`ChiefOfStaff_Autonomy.js`)
**GRADUATED AUTOMATION**

**Autonomy Levels:**
- **L0 - Human Only:** Human performs action (financial decisions)
- **L1 - Suggest:** AI suggests, human approves (email responses)
- **L2 - Auto Review:** AI executes, human can undo (routine acks)
- **L3 - Auto Notify:** AI executes, human notified (scheduling)
- **L4 - Full Auto:** No notification needed (spam filtering)

**Trust System:**
- Actions build trust score (0-100)
- Successful executions increase autonomy
- Rejections decrease autonomy
- Automatic level upgrades/downgrades

**Key Functions:**
- `checkActionPermission()` - Check if action can auto-execute
- `executeWithAutonomy()` - Execute with rules
- `undoAction()` - Undo within review window
- `updateTrustScore()` - Learn from outcomes
