# Direct Mail Campaign Plan: "Won't You Be My Neighbor"

**Prepared by:** Social Media Claude
**Date:** 2026-01-16
**Campaign Launch:** February 2026 (Pre-Spring CSA Push)

---

## Campaign Overview

### Objective
Acquire 20+ new CSA members through targeted direct mail to high-value Pittsburgh neighborhoods.

### Strategy
Use EDDM (Every Door Direct Mail) to blanket target neighborhoods with a warm, community-focused postcard driving traffic to a dedicated landing page.

### Target
- **Households:** 1,000
- **Response Rate Goal:** 4% (40 responses)
- **Conversion Goal:** 50% (20 new members)
- **Revenue Target:** $10,000 (20 x $500 CSA)

## The Offer

### "A FREE WEEK" Discount Structure
Code: `NEIGHBOR` (single code, tiered discounts)

| Product | Minimum | Discount | Value |
|---------|---------|----------|-------|
| Veggie CSA (Full Share) | $600+ | **$30 off** | 1 week free |
| Veggie CSA (Half Share) | $300+ | **$15 off** | 1 week free |
| Floral CSA (All) | Any | **$20 off** | 1 week free |
| Add-ons | - | No discount | - |

### Why This Works:
1. **Clear value proposition** - "A FREE week of your subscription!"
2. **Tiered structure** - Encourages full share signup for bigger savings
3. **Single code** - Easy to remember: NEIGHBOR
4. **Trackable** - All NEIGHBOR code usage = direct mail attribution
5. **Excludes add-ons** - Protects margins on extras
