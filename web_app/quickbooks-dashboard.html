QuickBooks Setup Wizard

Owner Action Required: Follow these steps to connect QuickBooks to Tiny Seed Farm OS.

1 Find Your Company ID (Realm ID)

1. Log into QuickBooks Online
2. Look at the URL in your browser address bar
3. Find the number after companyId=

Example URL: https://qbo.intuit.com/app/homepage?companyId=123456789012345
The Company ID is: 123456789012345

This is a 15-digit number from your QuickBooks URL

2 Get Client ID & Client Secret

1. Go to developer.intuit.com (sign in with your Intuit account)
2. Click "My Apps" in the top menu
3. If you have an app, click on it. If not, click "Create an App" → "QuickBooks Online and Payments"
4. Go to the "Keys & credentials" section
5. Use the Production Keys (not Sandbox)
6. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret

This will be stored securely in the backend

3 Add Redirect URI (Important!)

In your Intuit Developer App settings, add this Redirect URI:

https://script.google.com/macros/s/AKfycbyMDydZxlRWRNw3BTSU_tXMgw3R6tYK0zN6CdlG8RjFlFCk9_NKMuHrOACTVlmUvMdE/exec
This allows QuickBooks to redirect back to Tiny Seed Farm OS after authentication.

4 Required Scopes

Make sure your app has these scopes enabled:
• com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting - Access accounting data
• com.intuit.quickbooks.payment - (Optional) Payment processing

These should be enabled by default for QuickBooks Online apps.
